About

On this site I am going to tell you a little bit about myself.

I am a Junior at the Unversity of Michigan in the School of Information. This program intrigued me because it is the intersection of people, information and technology. When I first came to school here I was thinking about majoring in either Psychology or Computer Science, therefore the School of Information was perfect for me because it allowed me to combine my interests. I get to use my coding background to work with people and help them with whatever is asked of me.

I am majoring in Data Analytics. This allows me to formulate questions that are important to stakeholders and develop solutions in data. I enjoy being able to look at a messy dataset and be able to work on it and create visualizations that will clearly provide answers to the problems I am trying to solve.

I am also minoring in Entrepreneurship and learning business basics to be able to foster my creativity and learn how to make my ideas successful.
